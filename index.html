About

My Name is Carlos Torres and I was born and raised in Queens, N.Y. I served in the Navy for 9 years getting the opportunity to work, learn and travel. I worked as an electrician onboard an aircraft carrier for about five years and switched to Naval Corrections for the remainder of my career. Once I finished my enlistment I enrolled in the Web Development program at Seattle University and landed a temporary job as a Web Applications Developer at the University. Web development is a field in which you never stop learning which motivates me to always seek opportunities in which I can apply my skills as well as learn.

Proficient in HTML, CSS & Javascript. Experience with Bootstrap and Vue.js framework. Familiar with Adobe Creative Suite to create wireframes and UI design.
